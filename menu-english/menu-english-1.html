'NUNU' is the job of rescuing a child left in the vehicle.

NUNU is a manufacturer that makes child passenger safety device. In order to prevent continuous child lock-up not only in Korea but also around the world, we have developed an 'i-box' that can save the child through motion detection.

As a company that always strives to prevent accidents with the aim of developing the best safety system for the weak, we will make efforts to make products to prevent accidents involving children so that they can immediately rescue children who have been left in the vehicle by accident by drivers.
