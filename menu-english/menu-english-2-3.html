Motion Senser

If multiple Motion Sensors are installed inside the vehicle, when motion is detected, data is transmitted to the Main Control Box and the vehicle clock and emergency light flash to inform the outside that the child is trapped.
It tells you that you are trapped and helps you to rescue your child before a major accident occurs.
The Motion Sensor has different installation location and quantity depending on the structure of the vehicle, and it is designed to check the small movement of the child by using the Motion Sensor.
